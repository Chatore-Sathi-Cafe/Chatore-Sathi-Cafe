## 🍕 Our Signature Menu

Discover a wide variety of mouth-watering dishes crafted carefully to satisfy every single craving.

### 1. Artisanal Pizzas
Oven-fresh crusts layered with signature tomato sauce, premium aromatic herbs, and standard melting mozzarella cheese.
* **Classic Margherita Pizza:** Pure double mozzarella bliss over a rich traditional tangy sauce.
* **Farmhouse Special Pizza:** Packed with freshly diced capsicum, onions, tomatoes, and golden sweet corn.
* **Paneer Tikka Pizza:** Tantalizing cubes of tandoori marinated cottage cheese with a dash of smoky makhani gravy.
* **Spicy Jalapeño & Corn Pizza:** Fire up your taste buds with hot jalapeños perfectly balanced by sweet corn.
* **Chatore Sathi Special Loaded Pizza:** Our ultimate masterwork—heavily loaded with double cheese and every premium topping available in our kitchen.

### 2. Gourmet Pastas
Authentic Italian recipes reimagined with a rich, delicious, and savory Indian twist.
* **Red Sauce Pasta (Arrabbiata):** Tossed thoroughly in a fiery, herb-infused spicy tomato sauce.
* **White Sauce Pasta (Alfredo):** Creamy, rich, and velvety sauce loaded with butter, cheese, and mushrooms.
* **Mixed Sauce Pasta (Pink Sauce):** The absolute sweet spot between tangy tomato sharpness and rich heavy cream.
* **Baked Cheese Pasta:** Loaded with fresh garden veggies, submerged in cheese, and baked to a perfect golden brown crust.

### 3. Heavy Burgers
Big, thick, and juicy patties resting between lightly toasted butter buns with premium fresh veggies.
* **Classic Aloo Tikki Burger:** Crisp golden potato patty coupled with crunchy onions, fresh tomatoes, and special burger sauce.
* **Veg Supreme Burger:** A heavy, multi-veggie seasoned patty layered with crisp lettuce and premium sliced cheese.
* **Cheese Burst Burger:** A unique crispy patty that oozes a fountain of warm liquid cheese from your very first bite.
* **Paneer Grilled Burger:** A heavy block of fresh paneer marinated overnight in hot spices and grilled evenly.

### 4. Grilled Sandwiches
Perfectly toasted, crispy bread sheets packed with a premium variety of fillings.
* **Veg Grilled Sandwich:** Traditional street-style sandwich loaded with finely sliced spiced greens.
* **Corn & Cheese Sandwich:** Rich golden sweet corn kernels drenched completely inside bubbling melted cheese.
* **Paneer Tikka Sandwich:** Smoky grilled paneer strips paired with fresh mint chutney and chaat spices.
* **Club Sandwich (3 Layers):** A monumental triple-decker feast stuffed to the brim with assorted vegetables, multiple cheese layers, and signature house spreads.

### 5. Street-Style Momos
The absolute crowd-favorite dumplings served alongside our dangerously spicy house red chili chutney and cool mayonnaise.
* **Steamed Momos (Veg/Paneer):** Delicately wrapped, light, and incredibly juicy dumplings filled with classic seasoned stuffing.
* **Fried Momos:** Golden-fried to crisp perfection for that perfect crunchy texture.
* **Kurkure Momos:** Coated thoroughly in a heavy crunchy batter and fried—our absolute best-seller!
* **Tandoori Momos:** Bathed in authentic tikka marination and char-grilled inside a tandoor for a rich smoky aroma.
* **Gravy Momos:** Sizzling hot dumplings dipped into a thick, smooth, spicy, and savory secret gravy base.

### 6. Chinese Fusion Corner
Authentic wok-tossed Indo-Chinese delicacies hitting the perfect balance of sweet, tangy, and pungent profiles.
* **Veg Hakka Noodles:** Classic high-flame tossed stir-fried noodles packed with crisp julienned vegetables.
* **Schezwan Noodles:** Fiery, intense noodles tossed completely inside our in-house pungent Schezwan chili paste.
* **Veg Manchurian (Dry/Gravy):** Deep-fried shredded vegetable balls simmered gently inside a rich, glossy soy-garlic sauce.
* **Chilli Paneer:** Golden paneer blocks tossed aggressively with fresh green bell peppers, red onions, and hot green chilies.
* **Veg Fried Rice:** Highly fragrant basmati rice tossed elegantly with finely chopped seasonal garden vegetables.

### 7. Traditional Kulhad Items
Relish the unforgettable, heritage-steeped earthy notes of cooking inside standard clay pots.
* **Kulhad Pizza:** A beautiful, chaotic mixture of pizza toppings, premium sauces, and heavy cheese pulling baked directly inside an authentic earthen clay pot.
* **Kulhad Pasta:** Deeply rich, saucy penne pasta baked with a heavy, thick layer of cheese inside an earthen vessel.
* **Kulhad Chai:** Perfectly prepared, slow-brewed milk tea infused deeply with organic cardamom and strong ginger.
* **Kulhad Coffee:** Super frothy, strong, and piping hot coffee boasting an incredible smoky and rustic undertone.

### 8. Quick Snacks & Starters
The perfect side nibbles to keep the conversations running.
* **French Fries:** Golden, extra-crisp potato strips served either classic salted or tossed in hot Peri-Peri spice dust.
* **Potato Wedges:** Thick, skin-on premium potato wedges seasoned expertly with fine herbs and aromatic garlic.
* **Cheese Corn Nuggets:** Small, bite-sized crunchy golden poppers housing an core of sweet corn and molten cheese.
* **Garlic Bread:** Toasted artisan Italian bread infused with premium garlic butter—available Plain, with Double Cheese, or Stuffed with golden corn and jalapeños.

### 9. Thick Shakes
Ultra-creamy, rich, and completely indulgent milkshakes whipped up fresh to beat the heat.
* **Classic Chocolate Shake:** Smooth, rich, and velvety cocoa blend paired with dark chocolate syrup drops.
* **Oreo Crunch Shake:** A heavy, thick blend prepared with real crunchy Oreo cookies and dairy cream.
* **KitKat Break Shake:** An absolute treat loaded heavily with broken KitKat bars and rich chocolate fudge.
* **Strawberry Shake:** A fruity, exceptionally smooth, and refreshing timeless classic.
* **Butterscotch Shake:** Sweet, caramelized butterscotch crunchies crushed into a rich, velvety base.
* **Cold Coffee with Ice Cream:** Rich, strong, frothy caffeinated blend capped off with a massive scoop of vanilla ice cream.

### 10. Refreshing Beverages
Crisp, cold mocktails and warm comforting brews prepared live.
* **Fresh Lime Soda:** Fizzy, sparkling soda mixed right to your liking—Sweet, Salted, or Mix.
* **Virgin Mojito:** The ultimate refresher containing muddled mint leaves, lime wedges, sugar, and crisp sparkling water.
* **Blue Lagoon:** A beautiful, cooling, sweet, and wonderfully tangy tropical blue mocktail.
* **Iced Tea:** Cold-brewed premium tea served chilled over ice blocks with a refreshing hint of lemon and mint leaves.
* **Hot Coffee / Masala Tea:** Our deeply comforting, homestyle warm brews designed to make you feel right at home.
