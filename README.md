## 🍽️ Our Detailed Menu - A Feast for Every Palate

We take pride in our diverse menu, prepared with love and the freshest ingredients. Explore our categories below:

### 🍕 1. Artisanal Pizzas
Our pizzas are baked to perfection with hand-tossed dough and premium mozzarella.
* **Classic Margherita:** The timeless favorite with rich tomato sauce and double cheese.
* **Farmhouse Special:** Loaded with fresh capsicum, onion, tomato, and sweet corn.
* **Paneer Tikka Pizza:** Topped with spicy marinated paneer and makhani gravy.
* **Spicy Jalapeño & Corn:** For those who love a little heat balanced with sweetness.
* **Chatore Sathi Signature Pizza:** Our "Everything" pizza—loaded with every topping we have and extra cheese.

### 🥟 2. Momos (Dumplings)
The king of street food, served with our signature spicy red chutney.
* **Steamed Momos:** Light and healthy, available in Veg and Paneer variants.
* **Fried Momos:** Golden-brown and crunchy on the outside, juicy on the inside.
* **Kurkure Momos:** Coated in a special crunchy batter—our best-seller!
* **Tandoori Momos:** Marinated in tikka masala and charred for that smoky flavor.
* **Gravy Momos:** Served in a rich, spicy, and creamy sauce.

### 🍵 3. Authentic Kulhad Items
The earthy taste of traditional clay pots makes everything taste better.
* **Kulhad Pizza:** Layers of pizza toppings and cheese baked inside a clay pot.
* **Kulhad Pasta:** Creamy pasta with a smoky, earthy aroma.
* **Kulhad Chai:** Traditional Indian tea infused with cardamom and ginger.
* **Kulhad Coffee:** Frothy, strong coffee served in an earthen cup.

### 🍝 4. Gourmet Pastas
Indulge in our saucy, creamy, and flavorful pasta dishes.
* **Red Sauce Pasta (Arrabbiata):** A spicy tomato-based delight.
* **White Sauce Pasta (Alfredo):** Velvety, creamy, and packed with cheese.
* **Mix Sauce Pasta:** The best of both worlds—tangy and creamy.
* **Baked Cheese Pasta:** Oven-baked with a thick layer of melted cheese.

### 🍔 5. Burgers & Sandwiches
Quick bites that are big on flavor.
* **Aloo Tikki Burger:** Classic spiced potato patty with fresh salad.
* **Cheese Burst Burger:** A patty that oozes molten cheese with every bite.
* **Paneer Grilled Burger:** Juicy paneer marinated in tandoori spices.
* **Veg Grilled Sandwich:** Perfectly toasted with a medley of veggies.
* **Club Sandwich:** A three-layered masterpiece with veggies, cheese, and spreads.

### 🍜 6. Chinese Fusion
Wok-tossed delicacies with the perfect Indo-Chinese balance.
* **Veg Hakka Noodles:** Stir-fried noodles with crunchy vegetables.
* **Schezwan Noodles:** For the lovers of spice and garlic.
* **Veg Manchurian:** Deep-fried vegetable balls in a tangy soy-based gravy.
* **Chilli Paneer:** Crispy paneer tossed with bell peppers and onions.
* **Veg Fried Rice:** Aromatic rice tossed with finely chopped garden veggies.

### 🍟 7. Snacks & Sides
The perfect companions to your main course.
* **French Fries:** Available in Classic Salted or Peri-Peri spice.
